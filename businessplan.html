Business Plan

Executive Summary
Gardening and Co.. is dedicated to keeping your lawn clean and gorgeous. The new grass snipper robot comes equipped with state of the art gardening tools to make your lawn from bleak to out-right beautiful. Our company is dedicated to giving customers the garden they want with the all new Snipper Bot. Made with high quality parts with the finest tools, we do our best to give our customers the product they want. Make your gardening dream come true with the Snipper Bot.
Long Term Growth
In this moment of time, Gardening and Co has been trying to find more investors for our gardening robot, the Snipperbot. Thanks to investors for the enthusiasm and belief of our mission, we have been able to collect around $7.2 million in funding from all kinds of investors from around the world. In one year, we plan to finish building our first market worthy robot model and release it soon after to an exclusive crowd. We will then do more research on the Snipperbot and receive user feedback so we continue to enhance the Snipperbot experience for few more years. Gardening and Co’s highly experienced marketing team will also be working on the advertisements for our bot during the span of those four years. A year later, or five years from now, Gardening and Co will proudly release the Snipperbot to the public. Making an estimated $18 million per year, Gardening and Co will be right on track to being a super company! Our goal is to revolutionize the art of gardening, one backyard at a time.

Operations
It take 2 weeks to complete one robot. We have four factories in space, and use automated robots to create our products. We employ over 200,000 employees who are split into programing, designing, marketing etc. We attract people who are either too lazy to tend to their garden or don’t have enough time to tend their garden; we fix their problem by letting them know that there is a robot to do that work for them and advertise through video ads put on TV channels that feature gardening and lifestyle.
